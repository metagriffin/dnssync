URL forwarding maps host names to URLs. You can use this to redirect your domain to a free web-hosting service, for example.

Enter an at-sign (i.e. "@") on the left to indicate a URL record for the root domain (e.g. "example.com"), or an asterisk (i.e. "*") to indicate a wild-card URL record for your domain.

When you have finished editing the domain information below, click on the [ next ] button.
